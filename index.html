PRiiSM Strategy Development Module
This module guides organisations through the complete strategy lifecycle — from environmental scanning and competitive analysis, through gap identification and objective setting, to departmental cascade and ongoing annual review. Each stage is supported by AI-assisted tools, structured analytical frameworks, and collaborative approval workflows that connect directly to the PRiiSM execution engine.
